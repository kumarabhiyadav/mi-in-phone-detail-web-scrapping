Redmi 9- 4GB RAM, Multi Task-KING!

Redmi 9 supports your efficiency to multitask with its 4GB RAM that delivers fast and smooth processing while gaming, online streaming and video calling all at once. Available in two storage variants- 64GB and 128 GB, the smartphone gives you great space to store high-resolution photographs clicked with the AI powered dual camera.
Available in three colours- Sky Blue, Sporty Orange and Carbon Black
• The high performance Helio G35, comes with HyperEngine Game Technology for smooth gameplay. The octa-core processor clocks up to 2.3GHz
• 5000mAh high capacity battery comes with enhanced battery lifespan ensuring 25% increased charge cycles.
• Redmi 9 comes with a large 16.58cm (6.53) HD+ IPS Display that delivers an immersive viewing experience
• AI scene detection enabled camera’s delivers clear and detailed pictures with 13MP primary camera and 2 MP Depth sensor at the rear and a 5MP selfie camera in the front
• Redmi 9 comes with Aura edge design that gives a sleek and bold look to the smartphone.
• Features the reading mode 2.0 with a TÜV Rheinland certified display that protects your eyes from harmful blue light
• Protected by P2i, the smartphone comes with splash-proof design to avoid damage from water spills
• The Redmi 9 gets added protection with corrosion proof ports, rubberized seals and 380V surge protection
